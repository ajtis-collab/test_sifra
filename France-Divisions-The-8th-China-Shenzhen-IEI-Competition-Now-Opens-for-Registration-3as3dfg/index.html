The 8th China (Shenzhen) Innovation & Entrepreneurship International Competition Is Now Open

August 2024 Official Announcement International Competition Innovation

The 8th China (Shenzhen) Innovation & Entrepreneurship International Competition, renowned as the “Olympics” of the innovation and entrepreneurship community in this heady summer of the Olympics, is officially back in action! Embracing the concept of “Fostering International Exchange and Cooperation, Sharing Opportunities for Innovation and Development.”

We are now inviting outstanding entrepreneurial projects from 13 overseas divisional sites across the globe: Melbourne (Australia), Toronto (Canada), Paris (France), Munich (Germany), Milan (Italy), Tokyo (Japan), Eindhoven (Netherlands), Barcelona (Spain), London (United Kingdom), Silicon Valley (USA), Boston (USA), Stockholm (Sweden), and Zurich (Switzerland).

With a total prize pool of 9.71 million RMB, individual contestants may win up to 1.75 million RMB, including competition awards and comprehensive landing support. This substantial reward underscores our commitment to empowering innovative ventures.

Beyond financial incentives, the competition offers industrial incubation, technology finance, and project acceleration services, ensuring that high-potential startups receive the resources and guidance necessary to scale their businesses.

There are seven categories available: New Generation Computing, Digitalization and Fashion, High-End Equipment Manufacturing, Green Tech and Sustainable Development, New Materials, Bio-Medical and Health, and Marine.

The online application deadline is 10 September. The France division final will be held face-to-face in Paris. Winners will advance to the global finals in Shenzhen, China, with all travel costs fully covered — a free trip to China.

Apply now via our website: https://lnkd.in/eSCQQdsA

For inquiries, please contact us via LinkedIn or email: assoctisfr@gmail.com
